# Credit Risk Data Mart Design

## Architecture Type
Star Schema (Banking Standard)

---

## Fact Table

### fact_loan_performance
One row = One loan transaction

- LoanID (Primary Key)
- CustomerID (Foreign Key)
- EmploymentID (Foreign Key)
- EducationID (Foreign Key)
- PurposeID (Foreign Key)

- LoanAmount
- InterestRate
- LoanTerm
- CreditScore
- Income
- DTIRatio
- Default (Target Variable)

---

## Dimension Tables

### dim_customer
- CustomerID (Primary Key)
- Age
- MaritalStatus
- HasDependents
- HasMortgage

---

### dim_employment
- EmploymentID (Primary Key)
- EmploymentType
- MonthsEmployed
- EmploymentBand

---

### dim_education
- EducationID (Primary Key)
- EducationLevel

---

### dim_loan_purpose
- PurposeID (Primary Key)
- LoanPurpose

---

## Business Use Cases

- Credit Risk Dashboard (Power BI)
- Loan Default Analysis
- Customer Risk Segmentation
- Portfolio Risk Monitoring

---

## Insight Capability

This structure allows analysis by:
- Income
- Employment Type
- Education Level
- Loan Purpose
- Risk Category
